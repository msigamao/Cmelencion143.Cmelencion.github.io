PROPOSAL HEARING

The findings of this study would serve as input. The findings and conclusion are to come up with clear approved guidelines on the implementation of the software so that it would be clear to see as to who are the people responsible for the kiosks. The kiosk should be transferred near the Registrars office to become part of the basic services of the latter. If budget warrants, acquiring an additional unit of kiosk is highly recommended to ease the build-up queues of students patiently waiting to have their turn to use the system. However, the Institute of Information Technology shall make an alternative version of the system such as web-based and/or mobile apps for students to be able to access the system from home or anywhere through Internet connection. Thus, these require purchasing a web server. The system needs to undergo refinement of the system to make sure the continued use. There will be a trained software in-charge who may be responsible for the registration of students to avoid inaccuracy of account details during the registration. At the same time the said personnel or student-personnel will serve as the official person to attend students query upon notice by the Registrars office.To maintain the records easier and support data repository for future reference.


The finding and conclusion of this research are to develop the Management Information System, to subdivide the sub- system such as Manage Enrollee Information, Assign Courses, Assign Subjects, and Processing Admission. Otherwise, the MCC must develop a web-based enrollment system that will help enhanced and existing enrollment process. To develop a system that supports distributed and centralized repository of the enrollment records. To help the registrar office in securing the data in a more efficient way. To implement an enhanced enrollment process that will lessen the enrollment turn around processing time. The system has the ability to save and edit students information. It generates students study load and get the total/overall number of students enrolled within the semester. Registrar can add edit and delete. To develop a prototype web application for the MIS Enrollment Module. Student can withdraw subjects and can re-enroll. The user can login to the system with their respective users roles. Administrator have total access to the system confirming the students who are currently registering add/or enrolling. Data is the lifeline of every educational institution. Right from the student turnover rate to the financial records, every piece of data that is/was ever associated with the college paves way for its development. The bigger the institution, the greater the volume of institutional data, which makes it challenging to manage on paper or even with excel files. A Management Information System or MIS is a central data repository capable of not only gathering, organizing, and storing student data but also processing and analyzing it and generating various reports from it.
